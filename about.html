The Rebel Origin Story

Brick Troopers was founded by two LEGO Star Wars builders who once spent an entire weekend recreating the Battle of Endor — only to find the Ewok Village sold out across three galaxies. From that harrowing weekend, a mission was born: no builder shall be left brickless.

We stock only LEGO Star Wars sets — battle packs to the colossal Ultimate Collector Series. If it has a minifigure and a lightsaber, we probably sell it.
